# Introduction to Databases

Welcome to the first chapter of our Database Management textbook! This chapter provides a foundation for understanding databases, their purpose, and their evolution.

## Chapter Overview

In this chapter, you'll learn:

- **What databases are** and how they differ from simple file storage
- **The evolution** of database systems over time
- **Different types** of databases and their use cases
- **Database Management Systems (DBMS)** and their role
- **Why databases are essential** in modern computing

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, you should be able to:

1. Define what a database is and explain its core components
2. Describe the historical development of database systems
3. Identify different types of databases and their characteristics
4. Understand the role and functions of a DBMS
5. Articulate the benefits of using databases over traditional file systems
